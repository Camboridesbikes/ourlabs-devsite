OurLabs

OurLabs was established in 2018 as a certified Benefit Corporation dedicated to creating web, AR, and mobile applications around Public Service, Citizen Science, and Sustainability. Our projects and products create unique experiences around community engagement and distributed autonomous research. Our focus is to create great applications that are fun and exciting. We provide backend support and technology to community engagement tools such as the Trybrary project. This will take the form of templates and modules that facilitate a science and data based approach to Disaster Response, Community Gardening, Public Play, Flow and Physical Health, and Nutrition and Longevity.
